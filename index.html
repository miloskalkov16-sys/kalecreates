Not sure which package fits? Drop me a message — I'll tell you what you actually need.

1

Discovery call

Free 30-minute conversation. I learn about your business, your audience, and what you want your brand to feel like. No pitch, no sales pressure.

2

Brand brief

I translate what we discussed into a written brief — positioning, mood, direction. You approve it before any design work starts.

3

Concepts presented

You receive 2–3 distinct logo directions, each presented in context. Real mockups, not white-background PDFs.

4

Refinement

We pick the strongest direction and refine it together. Colour, weight, spacing — every detail until it's right.

5

Final delivery

All files, all formats, full copyright. A brand you can actually use — on print, digital, signage, anything.
